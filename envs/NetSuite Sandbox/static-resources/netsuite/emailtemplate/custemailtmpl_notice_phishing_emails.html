Valued insightsoftware Customer,

This letter is to provide you notice of a recent email phishing scam involving insightsoftware.

The incident, which took place on November 29, 2019, may have resulted in your customer account name being shared in an Excel spreadsheet. insightsoftware immediately launched an investigation into this incident and has determined that only minimal business data (customer account name) was compromised and no personally identifiable information (i.e. phone numbers, contact names, email addresses, product history) was shared. Regardless, as a valued customer we want you to be aware of this incident and to know we take it seriously. Protecting your data is a top priority for insightsoftware, and in the 72 hours after this occurrence, we’ve taken additional security measures to limit the future possibility of it happening again.

Due to the nature of the incident, we believe it is possible that you could potentially receive a fraudulent email from a 3rd party that would instruct you to make a payment to insightsoftware in a different manner. We ask that you please follow these precautions when responding to any email regarding insightsoftware.
  • Confirm that the sender’s email is from the correct domain: “@insightsoftware.com”
    • Scammers will use very minor changes to an email form such as changing a single letter in the domain name, please keep this in mind when opening or responding to any emails.
  • Consult your Information Technology department about any phishing attempts.
  • Call us at +1 (919) 872 7800 ext. 4 or email us at accounting@insightsoftware.com to confirm payment instructions or to validate that any correspondences originated from our company.

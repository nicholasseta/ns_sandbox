Hello insightsoftware vendors,

Accounts Payable has updated the vendor payment policies and processes. Please see below for details.

What’s new!

  • Bill submission cut-off deadlines
  • Update to vendor payment timing (around the 7th and the 21st)

 

Why are we changing the process?

  • Every other week payment cadence caused confusion
  • To improve vendor experience and ensure timely payment

 

Key takeaways

  • See the attached 2022 calendar which includes bill submission cut-off dates to ensure timely payments. Any bills received after cut-off will be processed and paid in the following check run. Payment terms apply.


To facilitate timely payments of your bill(s) please be sure to:

  • Submit bills to payables@insightsoftware.com by Noon EST on the dates listed on the calendar.
  • Include the Purchase Order number on your bill
    • If you do not have a Purchase Order, please reach out to your vendor relationship manager to obtain one.
  • Ensure up-to-date tax documentation/information (W9, W8, VAT #, etc.) in on file.
    • If you have not submitted updated tax documentation for 2022, please send your tax documents to purchasing@insightsoftware.com as soon as possible.
  • Ensure we have current wire/ach payment information on file.



Please let us know if you have any questions regarding this policy change.
